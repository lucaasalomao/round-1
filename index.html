Round One game!

  1. Directions:
  2. 1º Use arrow buttons to move
  3. 2º If you get caught by any guard you'll go back to the start
  4. 3º You only have 30 seconds to cross the field
  5. 4º If there is a tree in front of the Doll, you're good to go. If don't, don't move or she will eliminate you.
  6. 5º If you cross the line you save yourself and win the game.
  7. 6º Have fun! Try to reach the line in the minimum amount of time :)
